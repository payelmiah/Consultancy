About Us


Mission

To deliver high-quality tax, regulatory, audit, and business advisory services that foster compliance, enhance operational efficiency, and support informed decision-making — empowering clients to grow confidently in an evolving and complex business environment.

Vision

To be a leading professional services firm, trusted by businesses for our integrity, insight, and ability to simplify complexity — empowering organizations to achieve excellence in compliance, performance, and sustainable growth.

What We Do

SN & Associates is one of the most trusted professional accounting and business consulting firms in Bangladesh. We provide a comprehensive range of services including Taxation & VAT, Audit & Assurance, Business Advisory, Legal Consultancy, Company Formation, and Trademark Registration.

Our firm is led by seasoned professionals with 12 to 15 years of leadership experience in Accounts, Tax, VAT, MIS, and Audit. Both founding partners are members of the Institute of Cost and Management Accountants of Bangladesh (ICMAB), with proven expertise in both public and private sectors — including senior roles in banking and corporate leadership. This depth of experience enables us to offer practical, strategic, and regulatory insights tailored to your business needs.

At SN & Associates, we serve a wide variety of clients — from top manufacturing companies and multinationals to SMEs, media, legal, and retail sectors. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of professional ethics, integrity, and confidentiality in every engagement.

To provide a seamless, one-stop experience, we have formed alliances with support service providers in legal, IT, credit rating, and branding sectors. This helps our clients reduce complexity and focus on what matters most — growing their business.

Our mission is to simplify complex business and regulatory requirements through smart, proactive solutions. Whether you're an individual entrepreneur or a large organization, we are here to deliver clarity, compliance, and confidence in every service we offer.
